Call for participants

The Cognitional Cognitive Science Group at the University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley) is looking for online participants for a brief experiment on how people reason about objects.

In this experiment you will be shown video clips of towers of building blocks, and you will judge whether they will fall down or not. You will complete 62 trials, which typically takes about 15 minutes.

The requirements are:

  • You are at least 18 years old,
  • You are not colorblind,
  • You are a fluent English speaker, and
  • You have not participated in a previous version of this experiment.

Note: the experiment should work in recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, and Safari, but it will not work in Internet Explorer. You must also have JavaScript and Flash enabled.

This task can only be completed once. If you have already completed this task before the system will not allow you to run again. If this looks familiar please release the hit so someone else can participate.
